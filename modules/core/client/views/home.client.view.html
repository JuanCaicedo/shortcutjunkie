Shortcut Junkie


The stackoverflow of neat keyboard tricks!

Learn keyboard shortcuts

Shortcut Junkie is your one stop shop for shortcuts from any application*.
No more needing to go look through their reference docs!

*We're still building out this site to include more and more applications. If we're missing the one you want, check out how to contribute!

Remember your favorites

Want to keep a shortcut for quick reference? Once you are logged in to Shortcut Junkie, you can mark a shortcut as favorite and then view all your favorites in one convenient list.
